# Brand Decision — SonicForge Live / Intergalactic DJs / DJ VANTA

Updated: 2026-05-01T07:14:07.511108+00:00

## Final naming hierarchy

Use all three names, but each has a clean job:

1. **SonicForge Live** — the platform/runtime/app.
   - What judges clone/run.
   - The local-first autonomous DJ/VJ control plane.
   - The permanent Hermes-native performance system.

2. **Intergalactic DJs** — the party/show/collective layer.
   - What the audience understands instantly.
   - The house-party / livestream / festival wrapper.
   - The expandable universe: more autonomous DJs can join later.

3. **DJ VANTA//SonicForge** — the first performer/entity.
   - VANTA = Virtual Autonomous Nocturnal Transmission Artist.
   - The first autonomous AI DJ inside the Intergalactic DJs collective.
   - The voice/persona that talks, selects, mixes, guides visuals, and reads the room.

## Recommended public-facing sentence

**Intergalactic DJs presents DJ VANTA, powered by SonicForge Live: the first Hermes-native autonomous AI DJ/VJ for local house parties, livestreams, clubs, and future festival stages.**

## Why this is strongest

- **Intergalactic DJs** is fun, memorable, party-ready, and expandable.
- **SonicForge Live** sounds like serious creative infrastructure, not a gimmick.
- **DJ VANTA** gives the demo a living entity/persona.
- Together they solve three audiences:
  - Hackathon judges: SonicForge Live / Hermes-native autonomous performer.
  - Party people: Intergalactic DJs.
  - Story/character: DJ VANTA.

## Visual tone

- Intergalactic rave flyer meets terminal command center.
- Mad-scientist AGI notes over neon VJ glass.
- House-party friendly: fun, accessible, not corporate.
- Safe badges: local-first, no surprise cloud, no surprise stream.

## Copy blocks

### Short pitch

Intergalactic DJs is a local-first autonomous AI party system. DJ VANTA plans the set, writes the talk breaks, cues visuals, models beatmatching/EQ/phrasing, and can hand off to ComfyUI, Modal, RunPod, Resolume, TouchDesigner, OBS, or RTMP only when the operator arms those lanes.

### Hackathon pitch

Hermes is not just chatting — Hermes is operating a persistent creative performer. SonicForge Live packages Hermes memory, tools, skills, cron autonomy, safety gates, and local verification into DJ VANTA, the first Intergalactic DJ.

### House-party pitch

Open a browser, choose a vibe, and let DJ VANTA run a safe local intergalactic set: music plans, visual cues, MC breaks, and a timeline you can capture in OBS or route to your VJ stack.
