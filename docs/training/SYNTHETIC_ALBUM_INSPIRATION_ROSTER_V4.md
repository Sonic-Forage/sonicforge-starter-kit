## New creative direction

The album is now a map of the ecosystem that inspired SonicForge Live: ComfyUI, open-source AI models, weird internet AI folklore, public AI leaders, Cardano/Web3 builder culture, and the people teaching others how to build.

This is not a diss album and not a fake endorsement album. It is a tribute/commentary/forkable-builder mythology album.

## Recommended umbrella genre

```text
SonicForge glitch-rave: cyber-rave, glitch-hop, future jazz, warehouse electro, melodic techno, progressive house, broken-beat interludes, spoken-word AI MC hooks, and clean festival low-end.
```

Why this works:

- Glitch-hop/future-jazz gives Purz and creator/community tracks personality.
- Cyber-rave/warehouse techno keeps the album coherent and trainable.
- Progressive/euphoric house supports the human/care tracks.
- Broken-beat and glitch textures fit ComfyUI node graphs, AI models, code, and internet folklore.
- It avoids sounding like one named artist.

Dataset note: Keep most tracks in the 124–132 BPM family, with only a few intentional exceptions, so the future LoRA learns a coherent SonicForge sound.
