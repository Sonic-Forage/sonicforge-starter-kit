# DJ VANTA synthetic album — builder mythology roster V6

Status: planning only. No audio generation, model download, GPU job, provider call, upload, ticketing, event promotion, or training started.

## What changed from V5

This version follows the deeper roster direction:

- `Digital Underground` becomes the origin story: declined by bougie AI rooms / wine-party gatekeeping, then building our own underground instead.
- ComfyUI is explicitly the heart/soul/heartbeat of the visual spell engine.
- Comfy Anonymous gets a creator myth track.
- Comfy's current org/CEO leadership gets a separate track so we honor both the anonymous creator and the people scaling the project.
- Purz gets a special glitch-jazz / educator / motion-artist track.
- Qwen becomes a major pillar track, not a side reference.
- Unsloth gets a training/fine-tune acceleration track.
- Claude/Anthropic gets a constitutional/brass/drumline track: discipline, boundaries, safety, and command structure without becoming pro-war propaganda.
- Kandi Protocol becomes about AI/agent PLUR handshakes and teaching people new rituals.
- Hydration / harm reduction remains a core cultural track.
- The people keep the room human remains the emotional closer.

### Qwen

Qwen is treated as a huge inspiration pillar: multilingual, question-answering, coding/math/tool/agent energy, open model family, builder-friendly.

### Unsloth

Unsloth AI is an open-source fine-tuning / RL / local-training tooling company. Public materials emphasize faster fine-tuning, lower memory, local training, broad model support, and making open-source training more accessible.

### Claude / Anthropic

Claude/Anthropic fits as a track about constitutional AI, discipline, guardrails, refusal/boundary tension, and the uneasy military/defense conversation around frontier AI. The sonic angle should be brass/drumline/command cadence, not literal military recruitment or weapons worship.
