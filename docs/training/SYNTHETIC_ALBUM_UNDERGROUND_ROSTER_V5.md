# DJ VANTA synthetic album — underground rave roster V5

Status: planning only. No audio generation, model download, GPU job, provider call, upload, ticketing, event promotion, or training started.

## Direction change

The sound does not need to be PG. This is rave / warehouse / underground / digital counterculture.

But the project still should not become an evasion machine or an unsafe-party generator. The best lane is:

```text
Underground in culture.
Permissioned in space.
Serious about exits, water, consent, sound, and shutdown.
```

The album should feel sweaty, adult, weird, glitchy, anti-bougie, and anti-velvet-rope — while the SonicForge OS stays safety-serious.

## Genre

Recommended umbrella genre:

```text
Digital underground glitch-rave: warehouse techno, glitch-hop, future jazz, industrial electro, broken-beat bass, cyberpunk rave chants, spoken-word AI MC breaks, and sunrise progressive house closers.
```

Dataset coherence rule:

- Most tracks should live around 124–132 BPM.
- Purz / Cardano Slim tracks can be funkier and more syncopated.
- Hydration / human closer can drop tempo or become warmer.
- Keep SonicForge sound design consistent: clean sub bass, glitch percussion, neon synths, warehouse drums, chantable hooks, no named artist imitation.
