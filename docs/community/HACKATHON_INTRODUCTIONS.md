# Hackathon Introductions

Use this as the first discussion prompt.

## Introduce yourself

- Name / handle:
- What are you building?
- AI DJ, radio host, visual agent, party tool, workflow adapter, or something stranger?
- What tools are you bringing? ComfyUI, audio, frontend, docs, hardware, design?
- What help do you want?
- What should stay closed/safe/private in your build?

3. Add one dry-run workflow card.
