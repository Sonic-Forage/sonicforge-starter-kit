Why it wins

Not just “AI makes a song.” A performer.

Most AI music demos stop at a generated asset. SonicForge Live demonstrates the job of a DJ/VJ: selection, timing, transition intent, talk breaks, visual cues, safety reminders, set memory, and clear operator approvals.

Open the deck, press Plan Next Continuous Segment, and DJ VANTA returns the next track sketch, MC line, visual spell, Deck A/B handoff, beatmatch plan, EQ moves, survival ping, culture cue, and honest program status.

Demo flow

What happens in the live demo

1. GuideOperator gives VANTA a party direction.
2. PlanPlanner builds Deck A/B handoff and transition metadata.
3. MockLocal WAV sketch + text MC cue, no paid provider.
4. VisualizeBrowser VJ renders visual spell/code-rain style cues.
5. VerifyHealth, safety, backend status, and manifest stay honest.
Real DJ brain

Beatmatch + phrase + EQ

The payload includes BPM compatibility, 16/32-bar phrase planning, bass swap, drop release, cue points, talk ducking, target LUFS, and an equal-power crossfader plan.

Dual-deck framework

Deck A / Deck B

Inspired by live DJ rigs: Deck A carries the current groove; Deck B is the incoming portal with crate selection, prompt stack, visual spell, and dry-run ComfyUI cue.

Visual spells

Text shaders now, ComfyUI later

The browser VJ handles code rain, subtitle spells, EQ bands, and readable rave-terminal cues. ComfyUI becomes the opt-in visual/media workflow engine later.

Rave Survival Kit

Party care as a feature

Hydration, earplugs, buddy checks, consent reminders, chill zone, exits, and human override. Practical community-care copy only — no medical or drug-use instructions.

Culture layer

History is part of the system

DJ VANTA is built to respect the lineage: disco, house, techno, warehouse parties, VJ culture, sound systems, peer support, and local scenes. The AI is framed as a guest in the room, not the inventor of the room.

Red lines

What VANTA must not do

  • No drug dosing or ingestion instructions.
  • No medical diagnosis or emergency replacement.
  • No hidden microphone/crowd recording.
  • No public stream or cloud GPU without approval.
  • No pretending mock audio is a rendered continuous mix.
